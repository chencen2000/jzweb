ABOUT JEAN

Some people launch their career when success is nearly certain, but Coldwell Banker Realty agent Jean Zhu took a different approach. Already an accomplished hi-tech professional in Fortune 500 company, she found herself shopping for property in the middle of a deep recession. Frustrated by the service she received, Jean responded with what has become her signature approach: Do the research, pay attention to the details, ask hard questions, negotiate for the best possible outcome and then, at last, celebrate your success.

Jean has lived in the beautiful East Bay for over 17 years. Affiliated with the Danville office of Coldwell Banker Realty, she represents sellers, buyers and investors in San Ramon, Danville, Dublin, Pleasanton, Livermore and Fremont. Her commitment to the community, where her children attend local schools, and her commitment to her clients assure Jean of a steady stream of business. Sellers are assured of a lightning-fast sale via Jean’s skills with interior design, staging, and comprehensive marketing. As her clients note, she understands exactly what is required to make a fine property even more special, and she is known for the care she takes with each listing. Along with her friendly, “can-do” attitude, Jean’s fluency in English and Mandarin Chinese have broadened her client base. To learn more about Jean Zhu’s responsive, reliable real estate service, contact her today.
